You can now park your car safely and securely with us at Gatwick, Heathrow, Stansted, Southend, Southampton, Birmingham and Edinburgh airports.

If you have used Maple Parking Parking before at Gatwick, you’ll know that there is no easier, safer, more convenient and cost effective way to park your car at the Airport, using our meet and greet service. If you perhaps live nearer to the other airports, have used other parking methods at Gatwick, or have never thought to park at the airport before; we’ll let you know why parking with Maple Parking Parking could save you time, money and stress.

If you’re not familiar with who we are, here’s a brief outline of what you can expect from Maple Parking Parking at your airport.

  • The easiest and most convenient way to park at the airport
  • Safe and extremely secure airport parking in monitored car parks
  • Airport parking official approved operator
  • Fully insured, uniformed, ID checked staff
  • Secure online booking / live instant quotes
  • Competitive prices with a price match promise

The meet and greet service is simple and easy for our customers;

  • Book online by telling us what date / time you arrive and depart.
  • Arrive at the departure terminal in your car and meet our professional representative, who’ll conduct a quick check of your car, and then take it to our super secure car parks for you.
  • You are now free to simply walk into departures with your luggage, no stress.
  • One call when you land, and your driver will bring your car to the terminal to meet you.

We take the stress out of airport parking, the very best start to your travel, be it for business or pleasure. Our procedures are thoroughly and constantly checked, updated and adhered to, and our staff are highly trained so we can offer the most professional service to you at all times.

We expanded to other airports as we saw demand for easier parking methods, and felt that our particular meet and greet parking service would be extremely well received at the airports we’ve chosen. We’ll take a look at them all and explain why Maple Parking Parking meet and greet is working all over the UK.

If you’re local, you’ll know that traffic on these motorways is renowned for being bad, so getting a cab to the airport could work out to be much more expensive than parking your own car if you get stuck in traffic!
  • Our parking at Birmingham is proving extremely popular with families who prefer to drive their own car to the airport to make it easier for their little ones.
  • Getting public transport to Birmingham airport could be highly stressful and not worthwhile, especially if you have children or anyone in need of assistance for example, wheelchair user.
  • We continually check Birmingham airport parking prices, to ensure that you get the best parking deal and price, and we know that meeting our drivers right outside your terminal, is the most stress free way to start your journey.

  • Heathrow itself is huge, and parking around it will mean walking or getting a bus with all your luggage.
  • Our meet and greet parking at Heathrow has been extremely successful and popular so far, as we offer one of the most competitive meet and greet airport parking option at Heathrow. Check our prices and price match promise.
