Meet & Greet Parking at Birmingham Airport

Maple Parking have been offering reliable airport parking options at Birmingham airport for many years. Our meet and greet parking at Birmingham is the most convenient and stress free way to park at Birmingham Airport, and offers a low cost, convenient way to start your travel, be it business or pleasure.

OUR Advice & Guides

What are the perks of Maple Parking’s Birmingham Airport Meet and Greet?
  • You can drive right up to the terminal
  • You’ll avoid the hassle of finding a parking space
  • No waiting around for transfers to the terminal
  • You’ll save substantial amounts of time
  • Your vehicle will be protected 24/7
Why should you consider the Birmingham Airport Meet and Greet?

Make traveling easier for yourself with Maple Parking’s Birmingham Airport Meet and Greet service. Why waste time and energy fretting over parking spaces, terminal transfers and the safety of your vehicle if you don’t have to? With our VIP holiday parking service, all you have to do is drive to the terminal.

We’ll meet you there, check your car over and once that’s done, that’s it! You can make your way to the check-in while our chauffeurs drive your car to our fully protected car park where it will be guarded until your return. When you arrive back at Birmingham Airport after your travels, you’ll find your vehicle waiting for you right where you left it. Forget driving aimlessly through the long-stay and short-stay car parks trying to find a space, forget waiting around for a transfer to the terminal, and forget worrying if thieves or vandals have found your car while you’re away; this excellent service offers you all the convenience of valet parking at a far more reasonable rate.

Because of the time you’ll save with the Birmingham Airport Meet and Greet service, it’s perfect for anyone on a tight time-frame – and because you can drive right up to the terminal, it’s also ideal if you have lots of luggage or are travelling with young children.

How does the Birmingham Airport Meet and Greet service work?

Once your holiday parking with Maple Parking has been booked and confirmed, give us a call when you’re 15 minutes away from the airport. Then make your way to the designated meeting point which is within the Valet Parking Car Park; here you’ll be met by your chauffeur in a Maple Parking uniform.

Our staff will then photograph your vehicle and check it over for any damage while verifying the details of your return. Once this is complete, you’re then free to make your way to the terminal while we drive your vehicle to our protected car park.

When you arrive back at Birmingham Airport after your travels, give us a call when you’ve got all your luggage and are ready to drive home. We’ll then drive your vehicle right back to where you left it and hand the keys back over to you. It’s as simple as that!

Further information
  • Meeting point in short stay car park
  • Disabled facilities and parking spaces
  • All Maple Parking staff are CRB checked
  • All Maple Parking staff are comprehensively insured

Once you have booked your Maple Parking holiday parking, you will be emailed more detailed instructions.

All Maple Parking carparks are fenced, monitored by CCTV and patrolled by our security team 24/7.

When specifying what time you’d like to be met by your chauffeur, do take into account the fact that our staff must give your vehicle a thorough check-over before you can leave, so please allow time for this.

You must have a safe vehicle that is in possession of valid road tax and a valid MOT certificate in order to participate in our Meet and Greet service. If you don’t, we won’t be able to legally drive your car and you won’t receive a refund.

About Birmingham Airport

Birmingham Airport is the seventh busiest airport in the UK, with nearly ten million passengers coming through the airport in 2014. The airport offers both domestic and international flights, with approximately 50 airlines flying 140 direct routes. Birmingham Airport has several car parks offering short-term and long-term holiday parking, but none of their car parks can guarantee the safety of your vehicle.

Driving Directions to Birmingham Airport

Birmingham Airport is positioned right in the centre of the UK Motorway network off Junction 6 of the M42, so it’s easy to get to no matter which direction you’re coming from.

  • From the north-west take the M6, M6 Toll, M42
  • From the north-east take the M1, M42
  • From the east take the M6, M42
  • From the south-east take the M40, M42
  • From the south-west take the M5, M42
