]]> If you’re travelling from London Luton airport, the most convenient way to park your car is ‘Meet and Greet’ airport parking.  Simply drive to the terminal where you will be met by your fully insured driver, unload your bags and pass over the keys.

It really is that simple. Easy!
All the work is done for you with Luton Meet and Greet parking; your driver will be waiting for you on arrival at the terminal, leaving you free to check in for your flight. They’ll check over your car, before taking it to our PARK MARK approved car park, where it will remain safely for the duration of your stay.

We have been parking cars for over 26 years, and to give you additional peace of mind, all of our car parks are certified as assessed by the police, are extremely secure and have been awarded the Park Mark Safer Parking® accreditation.

When you arrive back at the airport, head over to our meeting area, collect your keys and return to your car, which the driver will have waiting for you.
