To get the best price, we always recommend you pre-book as early as possible, this will give you the best chance of securing a spot at a lower price, before prices go up and availability reduces.

Why Choose Meet and Greet Airport Parking?

Are you a frequent business flyer? Do you fly from the UK regularly? Are you going on holiday soon with your family, friends or sports / school group? If so, you need to know the name Maple Parking. Many passengers use expensive means like taxis to get to the airport, or park their car in overpriced onsite airport parking spaces far from the terminal. Others struggle with luggage and / or children on public transport to try and save a little money. The good news is, you can now drive straight to the departure terminal in your own car, and let us park your car for you, without breaking the bank.  Meet and Greet Airport Parking, is quick, easy and the only way to park.

The UK’s largest independent meet and greet airport parking provider, Maple Parking has been taking the airport parking industry by storm for over 22 years. Committed to value for money, trust and peace of mind, we pride ourselves on our customer service, making clients come back to us over and over again. All our staff are fully and regularly trained, extremely experienced in the parking industry and our drivers are all CRB checked, fully insured and most importantly, courteous and polite.

Our meet and greet parking is perfect for;

  • Families – who don’t want to haul their luggage and children onto public transport, or into taxis without baby seats. They can easily pack their own car, drive from home, straight up to the departure terminal. No transfers, or walks from car parks needed.
  • Disabled clients – who have specialist cars for their needs. It is important for these customers especially, that they drive their own car as close to the terminal as possible, and meet the airport assisted service staff.
  • Elderly flyers – Walking from car parks in our harsh UK weather, or getting airport shuttles, can be stressful and unnecessary. We serve 1000’s of elderly passengers who love the ease and comfort of our meet and greet parking.
  • Frequent Business travellers – These regular flyers make up a large portion of our clientele. Especially in London, businessmen and women, like to drive their own cars to the airport as, not only is it cheaper than paying London parking prices, but it’s the quickest, most convenient way to park at the airport. When you are flying so regularly, saving that time and money makes a huge difference to stress levels!

We provide not only meet and greet parking, but airport park and ride and a number of valet parking options too. Meet and greet airport parking is our forte, and due to our competitive low prices and deals, it’s by far our most popular parking option. The fact that you can drive your car straight to departures, drop it off with us, let us take care of the secure parking, and meet us outside arrivals on your return, makes it the easiest and most convenient way to park at the airport. If you want your car clean and shining on your arrival, choose one of our valet options, which start from just £9.95.

Our aim is to provide holidaymakers and business flyers alike, the biggest choice and lowest prices for their Airport parking needs. We serve 7 of the largest UK airports like Birmingham, Gatwick, Heathrow and Stansted with our Maple Parking branded parking options, as well as offering best price guarantee for all other parking products on our airport parking search engine, serving over 26 UK airports.

All our car parks are extremely safe and secure, as car safety is our top priority. All car parks are private property, fully fenced, have CCTV and our own security staff watching them closely, night and day. Many airport parking options seem to jeopardise security as the price reduces; we are dedicated in offering the most competitive prices, whilst maintaining the safest, most secure airport parking.

We constantly research the market and update our prices accordingly, to ensure that we are the most competitive company in the airport parking industry. If you are looking to save even more money, fly regularly or know when you are planning to fly; booking in advance could save you up to a massive 60%! Pre-book your parking and save. The more in advance you book, the more you save, and with our already low prices, you could be parking at the airport for literally next to nothing.
