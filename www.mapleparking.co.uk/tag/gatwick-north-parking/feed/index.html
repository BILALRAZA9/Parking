We welcome a huge number of travellers from Gatwick North Terminal each year – some travelling for business, and many others with family and friends for pleasure.
Low cost, stress free Gatwick North Terminal Airport Parking.

Maple Parking offer a very convenient meet and greet airport parking service straight from Gatwick’s North terminal, which is ideal if you are short of time, have many bags, or are travelling with small children – hassle free, quick and easy.

So how does it work?

You simply arrive at the terminal, and your Maple Parking driver will be waiting.
Maple Parking is located within an area of the short stay car park offering the closest meet and greet facility at Gatwick.

Our fully insured driver will conduct a quick check of your car, Inc. photographs, and then take your keys before driving it to our secure car park.

You are then free within a few minutes to make your way to the departures terminal, and enjoy your flight, with no fuss.

Car Clean at Gatwick North

This option allows travellers to park with all the benefits plus return to a sparkling clean car. There are a number of car cleaning options, from a simple exterior clean and internal hoover, to a full gold valet. Take a look at our valet packages here.
Please book your clean along with your parking, or alternatively, speak with your driver on arrival.

Gatwick North Terminal – things to remember.

The North Terminal has the self-check-in kiosks located near the standard check-in desks.
Arrivals is located on the ground floor. You can check arrival times online.

In this terminal, you can use this service if you are flying with:
• easyJet
• Flybe
• Norwegian
• Virgin Atlantic Airways
• WestJet

Special Assistance
There is a dedicated lounge at Gatwick North terminal for those with special assistance requirements, and this is located in the centre of the departures hall.
Toilets
You will find male, female and disabled/accessible toilets on both levels, whilst baby changing facilities are available only on the upper level.
