Meet and Greet Gatwick South

We are proud to be the unrivalled choice for meet and greet parking at Gatwick’s South Terminal. Whether you need to park at Gatwick South as a business traveller, a disabled traveller, as part of a family or team, our valet / meet and greet parking service is the easiest, fastest and most convenient way to park at Gatwick.

As a Gatwick Approved Operator, you can rest easy while you are away, in the knowledge that your car is in safe hands. Furthermore, the Approved Operator Scheme only uses those parking companies who’ve earned the Buy With Confidence Accreditation and the Park Mark Award. Maple Parking are proud of this accreditation.

The south terminal is the oldest terminal at Gatwick Airport, and is where most arrivals and departures are dealt with. We offer parking options to suit all requirements and budgets, including Long Stay and Short Stay. All car parks are CCTV monitored.
